Let's fix the CFAA.

The Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (aka CFAA) determines everything that’s legal and illegal on the Internet - but it was written in 1986, before most Americans started interacting online. Law enforcement and government officials have used the CFAA to threaten and jail innovators and activists for years - and it’s time we bring it into the right century.
