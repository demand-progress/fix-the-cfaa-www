More Info:

The Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (aka CFAA) determines much of that’s legal and illegal on the Internet - but it was written in 1986, before most Americans started interacting online and before the World Wide Web was even invented. Corporations, law enforcement, and government officials have used the CFAA to threaten and jail innovators and activists for years - and it’s time we bring it into the right century.
